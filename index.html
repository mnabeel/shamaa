About This Documentary

The Scientific Revolution represents one of the most transformative periods in human history. Between 1543 and 1687, brilliant minds challenged ancient authorities and established the foundations of modern science through observation, experimentation, and mathematical reasoning.

This documentary explores how pioneers like Copernicus, Galileo, Kepler, and Newton revolutionized our understanding of the cosmos, established the scientific method, and laid the groundwork for the Age of Enlightenment.

Their courage to question established dogma and their commitment to empirical truth continue to inspire scientists and thinkers today.
